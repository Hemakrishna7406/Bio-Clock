## 🚀 The Mission
Every year, 1.3 billion tons of food are wasted. **Bio-Clock** isn't just a tracker; it's an intelligent medical triage for your kitchen. By combining the raw power of **Amazon Nova Pro** (Generative AI) with symbolic logic, we predict spoilage before it happens, keeping your kitchen sustainable and your wallet full.

## 🧠 The "Neuro-Symbolic" Edge
Bio-Clock uses a dual-layer intelligence system:
* **The Neural Layer:** Computer vision powered by Amazon Nova Pro identifies food types and assesses visual freshness.
* **The Symbolic Layer:** A rule-based logic engine that calculates shelf-life based on environmental variables and historical data.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Backend & Orchestration:** AWS Lambda & API Gateway
